Subho Sankar Banerjee

Software Engineer @ Google

I am a Software Engineer at Google, where I work on building reliable hardware systems for machine learning and AI.

I received a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, advised by Ravishankar K. Iyer. My dissertation research aimed to improve the performance, resilience, and ease of management of large-scale computer systems using machine learning.
